desc:  Afghan Kush was developed in the mountains of Hindu Kush, near the border between Afghanistan and Pakistan. It\'s frequently used as a source for Charas and Afghani hash. Seeds were made available by the White Label Seed Company. The buds of this strain are heavy in resin, and are thick-topped and blunt. The leaves are wide, dark and green. This is a real Indica! A lot of users have reported a sweet, Pinesol-type smell from the strain, and it\'s popular amongst insomniacs and those needing pain relief. This is a great "beginner" strain as it\'s easy to grow, especially in wamrer climates.
